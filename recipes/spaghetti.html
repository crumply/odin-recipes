Spaghetti

Description

Don't get too excited, we're keeping it simple with this one.

Ingredients

  • 1 jar of Classico spaghetti sauce (flavor is up to you)
  • Angel hair pasta
  • Pre-made frozen meatballs

Steps

  1. Put frozen meatballs in air fryer and pour in jar of sauce.
  2. Set air fryer to manual pressure and set time for 15 minutes.
  3. Boil angel hair pasta for 4 minutes, then drain.
  4. Pour meatballs and sauce over noodles.
