Grilled Cheese

Description

Unlock the full potential of the classic grilled cheese sandwich.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
  • 2 slices Italian bread
  • 2 (1 ounce) slices white cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Spread one slice of bread with mayonnaise.
  2. Place bread mayonnaise-side-down into hot skillet.
  3. Add cheese.
  4. Spread other slice of bread with mayonnaise.
  5. Place mayonnaise-side-up on top of cheese.
  6. Cook until lightly browned on one side.
  7. Flip over and cook until cheese is melted.
